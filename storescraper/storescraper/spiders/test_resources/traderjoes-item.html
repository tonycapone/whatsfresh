Corn Tortillas

Trader Joe's Corn Tortillas are simple, traditional corn tortillas, crafted the old fashioned way, using stone ground corn, water, and a bit of lime. They feature the slightly chewy texture and hearty corn flavor that makes corn tortillas so appealing. They're made without preservatives, which we find pretty appealing as well. Our Corn Tortillas make delicious tacos - fill them with everything from our strips of Sirloin Steak with Chimichurri Sauce to Carnitas, or even pieces of Vegetable Masala Burgers for an entirely non-traditional taco. We've been known to make a grilled quesadilla by placing one directly on the grill, generously sprinkling with cheese, then topping with another tortilla - simple and delicious. And you might even cut them into triangles, dust with salt and bake for quick and delicious "homemade" tortilla chips. We're selling each 12 ounce package of about 12 tortillas for $1.49*, every day.

*Ounces, price and availability may vary by region. Currently Trader Joe's Corn Tortillas are $0.99- $1.49.
